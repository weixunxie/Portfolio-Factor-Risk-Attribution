# TSLA Q3 2024 Earnings Call Transcript (Excerpts)

## CEO Remarks — Elon Musk

"We had a record quarter for vehicle deliveries, producing over 470,000 vehicles and delivering over 462,000. This is a testament to our manufacturing excellence and the hard work of the Tesla team globally."

"On the robotaxi front — this is the most important product in Tesla's history. The Cybercab and Robovan we revealed in October represent our vision for the future of transportation. We plan to begin autonomous ride-hailing operations in 2025, starting in California and Texas."

"FSD Version 12.5 is a significant improvement over prior versions. The neural network architecture changes have reduced interventions meaningfully. We are on track for full autonomy. I am more confident than ever that we will achieve fully autonomous driving before the end of next year."

"The energy business is on fire. Megapack deployments are accelerating and we are seeing incredible demand from utilities and commercial customers. This is a multi-hundred-billion-dollar revenue opportunity."

## CFO Remarks — Vaibhav Taneja

"Q3 automotive gross margin excluding regulatory credits was 17.1%, up sequentially. This improvement reflects lower costs per vehicle, lower raw material costs particularly for lithium, and higher FSD revenue recognition."

"Energy generation and storage revenue was $2.4 billion, up 52% year over year, with segment gross margin of 30.5%. This is an exceptional margin profile that we expect to sustain."

"Total operating expenses declined sequentially as we continue to drive efficiency. We generated $2.7 billion in free cash flow this quarter."

## Q&A Session

**Analyst question on autonomous timeline credibility:** "You have given timelines for FSD and robotaxi before that were not met. Why should investors believe this time is different?"

**Elon Musk:** "I understand the skepticism. The honest answer is that the problem was harder than we originally appreciated. But the step change in capability from V11 to V12 — the shift to end-to-end neural networks trained on video — is qualitatively different. We are not extrapolating old progress curves. The technology is fundamentally better now. I am putting my credibility on the line again, and I believe we will deliver."

**Analyst question on China competition:** "BYD is outselling Tesla globally. How do you think about Tesla's competitive position in China specifically?"

**Elon Musk:** "BYD makes good cars, and we respect them. But we are not competing on price at the low end of the market. Our focus in China is the premium segment, where our brand, technology differentiation, and Supercharger network provide competitive advantages. We continue to gain share in that segment."

**Analyst question on CEO bandwidth:** "You are running Tesla, SpaceX, xAI, X, and now have significant political obligations. How should investors think about your bandwidth?"

**Elon Musk:** "I am very efficient with my time. Tesla is my largest personal investment and it gets a significant share of my attention. The work I am doing on AI at xAI is also directly relevant to Tesla's autonomous driving program — there are synergies. I would not be doing anything that I believed was harmful to Tesla."
